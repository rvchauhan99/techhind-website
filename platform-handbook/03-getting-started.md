# Getting Started

## Logging In

Open your organization URL (e.g. `https://demo.techhind.in`) and sign in with your email and password.

![Login screen](../assets/screenshots/auth-login.png){.hero}

## Navigation

The **sidebar** shows modules assigned to your role. Use **menu search** to find any screen quickly. The **notification bell** shows alerts; **global search** finds records across modules.

## Home Dashboard

Your landing page shows KPIs, sales pipeline, and recent orders — a daily snapshot for management and sales.

![Home dashboard](../assets/screenshots/dashboard-home.png){.hero}

## Typical First Steps

| Role | Start with |
|------|------------|
| Sales | Marketing Leads → Inquiry → Quotation |
| Operations | Confirm Orders → Fabrication & Installation |
| Warehouse | Stocks → Purchase Orders |
| Finance | Payment Outstanding → Reports |
| Admin | Company Profile → Users & Roles |
